    About Me

    Hello, I'm Ugo, a self-taught web designer and developer with hands on experience. I specialize in HTML, CSS, JavaScript, NodeJS, MongoDB, ReactJS, and ExpressJS, and I am passionate about creating beautiful, functional websites and web applications that solve real-world problems.

    In my years of experience, I have worked on various projects ranging from small business websites to complex web applications. I have gained valuable experience in project management, team collaboration, and problem-solving. My skills in HTML, CSS, and JavaScript allow me to create beautiful and responsive front-end interfaces that provide users with an excellent user experience.

    Moreover, I have extensive experience in back-end development using NodeJS, ExpressJS, and MongoDB. This enables me to create robust and scalable web applications that can handle high traffic and data-intensive processes.

    As a self-taught developer, I have a deep understanding of the latest web development trends and technologies. I am constantly learning and improving my skills, and I believe that continuous education is key to staying ahead in this fast-paced industry.

    My approach to web development is centered around collaboration and communication. I work closely with my clients to understand their unique needs and goals, and I am always happy to answer any questions and provide guidance throughout the development process.

    In addition to my technical skills, I also have a keen eye for design and aesthetics. I believe that a great website not only functions well, but also looks beautiful and reflects the personality and values of the brand it represents.

    If you're looking for a talented and dedicated web designer and developer to bring your project to life, I would love to hear from you. Let's work together to create something truly amazing!.

    On a lighter note, when I'm not coding, I enjoy exploring new technologies, photography and content creation, reading tech blogs, listening to good and passive music, and participating in online coding communities. I also love spending time with my family and taking part in outdoor activities.
